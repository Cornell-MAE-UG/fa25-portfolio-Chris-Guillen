An interactive offline study tool built for US Coast Guard Rules of the Road licensing exams (BDOC/OUPV). All 1,010 questions drawn directly from the official USCG test bank (Book 1, June 2001 Edition).

## Features

- **Practice Quiz** — 1,010 real USCG test bank questions, filterable by topic and ruleset (COLREGS, Inland, or both)
- **25-Question Timed Exam** — Simulates the BDOC exam format with a 30-minute timer and pass/fail scoring at 70%
- **Vessel Light Generator** — Top-down and side-profile views showing correct horizontal position and vertical height for all vessel types
- **Rule Reference** — Built-in summaries of Rules 2–35 with plain-English explanations and common exam traps
